## Question

What is malaria prophylaxis by region? Use the **"Malarone for Short Trips, Doxy for Long Trips, Chloroquine if Sensitive"** framework.

## Answer

### **Prophylaxis Regimens:**

| Drug | Dosing | Start/Stop | Regions |
|------|--------|-----------|---------|
| **Atovaquone-proguanil** (Malarone) | **250/100mg daily** | 1-2 days before → 7 days after | **Most preferred** (chloroquine-resistant areas) |
| **Doxycycline** | **100mg daily** | 1-2 days before → 28 days after | **Preferred for long trips** (cheap) |
| **Mefloquine** (Lariam) | **250mg weekly** | 2-3 weeks before → 4 weeks after | Alternative (neuropsych side effects) |
| **Chloroquine** | **500mg weekly** | 1-2 weeks before → 4 weeks after | **Only** chloroquine-sensitive areas (Central America, Caribbean) |

### **Drug Selection by Region:**

**Chloroquine-Resistant (Most of Africa, SE Asia, South America):**
- **First-line:** Atovaquone-proguanil (Malarone)
- **Alternative:** Doxycycline (cheaper, longer duration after)
- **Third-line:** Mefloquine (neuropsych side effects)

**Chloroquine-Sensitive (Central America, Caribbean, Middle East):**
- **Chloroquine** 500mg weekly

### **Special Populations:**

**Pregnancy:**
- **Chloroquine** (safe all trimesters) if sensitive region
- **Mefloquine** (safe 2nd/3rd trimester) if resistant region
- **Avoid:** Doxycycline, atovaquone-proguanil, primaquine

**Children:**
- **Atovaquone-proguanil** (weight-based, >5kg)
- **Chloroquine** (if sensitive region)
- **Mefloquine** (if >5kg)

## Key Points

### **Why Atovaquone-Proguanil (Malarone) Preferred:**
- **Short duration** after travel (7 days vs 28 for doxy)
- **Few side effects**
- **Effective** in chloroquine-resistant areas
- **Disadvantage:** Expensive

### **Why Doxycycline for Long Trips:**
- **Cheap**
- **Effective** in all chloroquine-resistant areas
- **Disadvantages:** 28 days after travel, photosensitivity, GI upset

### **Mefloquine Cautions:**
- **Neuropsych side effects** (anxiety, depression, psychosis, seizures)
- **Contraindicated:** Seizure disorder, psychiatric history
- Start 2-3 weeks before to assess tolerance

### **Clinical Pearls:**
- **Malarone:** Best for short trips (1-2 weeks)
- **Doxycycline:** Best for long trips (>1 month), cheap
- **Chloroquine:** Only if sensitive region (Central America, Caribbean)
- **All prophylaxis:** Continue after leaving endemic area
- **No prophylaxis is 100% effective** (use mosquito nets, DEET)
