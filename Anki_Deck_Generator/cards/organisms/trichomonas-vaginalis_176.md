## Question

How is trichomoniasis diagnosed and treated? Use the **"Frothy Green Discharge + Strawberry Cervix, Motile Flagellates → Metronidazole 2g × 1"** framework.

## Answer

### ***Trichomonas vaginalis* Overview:**

**Transmission:**
- **Sexually transmitted** (vaginal intercourse)
- **Most common non-viral STI** worldwide

### **Clinical Presentation:**

**Women:**

**Symptomatic (70%):**
- **Frothy, yellow-green vaginal discharge** (malodorous, "fishy" smell)
- **Vulvovaginal irritation:** Pruritus, dysuria, dyspareunia
- **"Strawberry cervix":** Punctate hemorrhages (colpitis macularis) - seen in 2-5% (pathognomonic)
- **Vaginal pH >4.5** (normally <4.5)

**Asymptomatic (30%):**
- **Can remain asymptomatic** for months

**Men:**

**Asymptomatic (90%):**
- **Most men asymptomatic** (carrier state)

**Symptomatic (10%):**
- **Urethritis:** Dysuria, urethral discharge (clear/purulent)
- **Rarely:** Prostatitis, epididymitis

### **Complications:**

**Women:**
- **Increased HIV transmission** (2-3× risk)
- **Pregnancy:** Preterm birth, low birth weight, PROM
- **PID** (facilitates ascending infection)

**Men:**
- **Prostatitis** (rare)

### **Diagnosis:**

**NAAT (PCR) - Preferred:**
- **Vaginal/endocervical swab, urine** (women)
- **Urine** (men)
- **Sensitivity >95%**
- **FDA-approved**, most accurate

**Wet Mount (Saline Microscopy):**
- **Motile flagellated protozoa** (pear-shaped, 10-20 μm)
- **Sensitivity 50-70%** (low, many false negatives)
- **Requires immediate examination** (motility lost within 30 minutes)

**Culture:**
- **Gold standard** (high sensitivity)
- **Rarely used** (takes 3-7 days)

**Rapid Antigen / DNA Probe:**
- **OSOM, Affirm** (point-of-care tests)
- **Sensitivity 80-90%**

**Vaginal pH:**
- **>4.5** (elevated, as in bacterial vaginosis)

### **Treatment:**

**Recommended (CDC 2024):**

**Single-Dose Therapy (Preferred):**
- **Metronidazole 2g PO × 1** (single dose)
- OR **tinidazole 2g PO × 1** (single dose)

**7-Day Therapy (Alternative):**
- **Metronidazole 500mg PO BID × 7 days**
- **Higher cure rate** (90-95% vs. 85-90% single dose), but compliance lower

**Treat Sexual Partners:**
- **Always treat partners** (even if asymptomatic)
- **Expedited partner therapy (EPT)** recommended (prescribe treatment for partner without exam)

**Abstain:**
- **Avoid sex** until patient + all partners treated and asymptomatic

**Test of Cure:**
- **NOT routinely recommended**
- **Retest at 3 months** (high reinfection rate, 17%)

**Pregnancy:**
- **Metronidazole safe** in pregnancy (all trimesters)
- **Treat symptomatic pregnant women** (may reduce adverse outcomes)

**Allergy to Metronidazole/Tinidazole:**
- **Desensitization** (no effective alternative)

**Persistent/Recurrent Infection:**
- **Metronidazole 500mg PO BID × 7 days** (if initial single dose failed)
- **If fails again:** **Metronidazole 2g PO daily × 7 days** OR **tinidazole 2g PO daily × 7 days**
- **Resistance testing** (available at CDC)

## Key Points

### **"Strawberry Cervix":**
- **Punctate hemorrhages** (colpitis macularis)
- **Pathognomonic** for trichomoniasis
- **Seen in only 2-5%** (do NOT rely on this finding)

### **Frothy Green Discharge:**
- **Classic description** (yellow-green, frothy, malodorous)
- **But discharge can be:** Yellow, white, or gray (NOT always green/frothy)

### **Vaginal pH >4.5:**
- **Elevated pH** (as in bacterial vaginosis)
- **Distinguishes from *Candida*** (pH <4.5)

### **Treat Partners:**
- **ALWAYS treat** (even if asymptomatic)
- **Reinfection common** if partners not treated (17% within 3 months)
- **Expedited partner therapy (EPT)** recommended

### **Metronidazole 2g × 1:**
- **Single-dose preferred** (compliance, directly observed)
- **7-day regimen** slightly more effective (but compliance lower)

### **Men Often Asymptomatic:**
- **90% asymptomatic** (carriers)
- **Test/treat if:** Sexual partner diagnosed, urethritis without gonorrhea/chlamydia

### **Increased HIV Risk:**
- **2-3× increased risk** of HIV acquisition/transmission
- **Treat to reduce HIV risk**

### **Clinical Pearls:**
- **Frothy yellow-green discharge** + vaginal pH >4.5 + pruritus
- **"Strawberry cervix"** pathognomonic (but rare, 2-5%)
- **Motile flagellates** on wet mount (low sensitivity, 50-70%)
- **NAAT (PCR)** most accurate (>95% sensitivity)
- **Metronidazole 2g PO × 1** (or tinidazole 2g × 1)
- **Treat partners** (EPT), abstain until treated
- **Retest at 3 months** (high reinfection rate)
