## Question

What are the four major **severe cutaneous adverse reactions (SCARs)** to antibiotics, and how do you distinguish them? Use **DAD-SAT** mnemonic for DRESS features.

## Answer

### **Four Major SCARs:**

| Reaction | Onset | BSA Involvement | Key Features | Mortality |
|----------|-------|-----------------|--------------|-----------|
| **DRESS** | 2-8 weeks | <10% | Eosinophilia, systemic organs, fever | 5-10% |
| **SJS** | 1-3 weeks | **<10%** | Targetoid lesions, mucosal involvement | 5% |
| **SJS/TEN overlap** | 1-3 weeks | **10-30%** | Progression of SJS | 15% |
| **TEN** | 1-3 weeks | **>30%** | Widespread epidermal necrolysis | 20-30% |

### **DRESS Syndrome (Drug Reaction with Eosinophilia and Systemic Symptoms):**

**DAD-SAT mnemonic:**
- **D**elayed onset (2-8 weeks after drug start)
- **A**trophy (skin desquamation, exfoliative dermatitis)
- **D**ermatitis (diffuse maculopapular rash, facial edema)
- **S**ystemic involvement (liver, kidney, lungs)
- **A**typical lymphocytes and eosinophilia (>1,000/μL or >11%)
- **T**emperature (fever >38.5°C)

**Type IV hypersensitivity (IVb): CD4+ cells + eosinophils**

### **SJS/TEN:**
- **Type IV hypersensitivity (IVc): CD8+ and NK cells**
- Targetoid skin lesions → blistering → epidermal detachment
- **Nikolsky sign** positive (skin sloughs with pressure)
- Mucosal involvement: oral, ocular, genital
- Rapid progression (hours to days)

## Key Points

### **Beta-Lactam SCARs:**
- **Most common culprits:** Cephalosporins > carbapenems > penicillins
- **Cephalosporins:** Most frequent cause of β-lactam SCARs
- **Carbapenems:** Associated with poor prognosis in SJS/TEN

### **Management:**
1. **Immediate drug cessation** (most important intervention)
2. **DRESS:** Systemic corticosteroids (prednisone 0.5-1 mg/kg/day)
3. **SJS/TEN:** Burn unit/ICU care, consider IVIG, cyclosporine
4. **Supportive:** Fluids, wound care, ophthalmology consult

### **Cross-Reactivity:**
- **AVOID ALL β-lactams** if SCAR to any β-lactam
- **Co-reactivity** common (cross-reaction to structurally unrelated drugs)
- Permanent allergy label - NEVER rechallenge

### **Mortality Predictors:**
- Extent of skin detachment (TEN > SJS)
- Age >40 years
- Cancer diagnosis
- Tachycardia
- Initial serum bicarbonate <20
