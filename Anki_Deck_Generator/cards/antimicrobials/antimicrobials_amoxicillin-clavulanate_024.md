## Question

What are the key clinical uses of **amoxicillin-clavulanate (Augmentin)**, and what are the appropriate doses for different indications? Use **BITES-CAP** mnemonic.

## Answer

**BITES-CAP mnemonic for amoxicillin-clavulanate uses:**

- **B**ite wounds (dog, cat, human)
- **I**ntra-abdominal infections (mild, community-acquired)
- **T**ooth/dental infections
- **E**ar infections (otitis media)
- **S**inusitis (acute bacterial)
- **C**ommunity-**A**cquired **P**neumonia (outpatient)

### **Spectrum:**
- MSSA (NOT MRSA)
- Streptococci (including *S. pneumoniae*)
- *H. influenzae*, *M. catarrhalis*
- Oral anaerobes (*Prevotella*, *Fusobacterium*)
- Enterobacterales (Î²-lactamase producers)

### **Dosing:**

| Indication | Dose | Duration | Rationale |
|------------|------|----------|-----------|
| **Sinusitis/otitis** | 875/125mg PO BID | 5-10 days | Standard dose |
| **CAP (outpatient)** | 875/125mg PO BID | 5-7 days | Non-severe |
| **CAP (high-dose)** | 2000/125mg XR PO BID | 7-10 days | Augmentin XR for resistant *S. pneumoniae* |
| **Bite wounds** | 875/125mg PO BID | 5-7 days (prophylaxis 3-5d) | Covers *Pasteurella*, *Eikenella*, anaerobes |
| **Diabetic foot** | 875/125mg PO BID | 1-4 weeks | Mild infections only |

## Key Points

- **High-dose amoxicillin (2000mg XR)** overcomes resistant *S. pneumoniae* (better lung penetration)
- **Avoid 250/125 or 500/125 dosing** for serious infections (inadequate amoxicillin)
- **GI side effects common:** Take with food, consider 875/125 BID over TID dosing
- **Does NOT cover:** *Pseudomonas*, MRSA, atypicals (*Mycoplasma*, *Legionella*)

### **Bite Wound Coverage:**
- **Dog/cat:** *Pasteurella multocida* (70% of infections), *S. aureus*, oral anaerobes
- **Human:** *Eikenella corrodens*, *S. viridans*, anaerobes
